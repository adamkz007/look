A PDF & EPUB reader built for research.

Read with a thumbnail sidebar, multi-page display modes, and smooth zoom controls. Highlight or underline with six colors, and keep everything organized in one workspace.

Markdown notes that stay fast.

More Features

🔒

Privacy First

All data stored locally by default. No cloud required, and no tracking during normal use.

📁

Collections & Tags

Organize with collections and color-coded tags. Add structure without locking your library into a rigid folder tree.

🔍

Search

Search by filename or content and jump straight to what you’re looking for in PDFs and notes.

📄

Smart Import

Drag-and-drop PDFs with automatic SHA-256 deduplication, metadata extraction, and OCR detection.

🎨

Six Highlight Colors

Annotate with yellow, green, blue, pink, orange, and purple. Give every color a semantic meaning.

📑

Note Templates

Start from blank, literature review, or meeting notes templates to keep writing consistent.

📱

iPadOS App

Work on iPad with a touch-optimized interface and feature parity with macOS.

📖

EPUB Support

Import and read EPUBs alongside PDFs, using the same organization and search workflows.

💻

Native macOS

Built with SwiftUI and PDFKit. Feels right at home alongside Finder, Spotlight, and your other Mac apps.
